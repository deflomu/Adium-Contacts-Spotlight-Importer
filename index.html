Adium Contacts Spotlight Importer

What?

This Adium plugin makes all your loved Adium contacts available in Spotlight! You can search your favorite contact with Spotlight and hit enter to start a conversation.

Feel free to report issues on Github or submit patches.

How?

Download the plugin for free, extract it and double click on the file. Adium will ask you if you want to install the plugin and you will answer "Yes, I will". A popup will appear and ask you if you trust us and you will answer "Yes, I do".

After installation it might take a second until the contacts appear in Spotlight.
